Katutura has 3 main programs:

Afterschool and Summer Programming for youth 5-13

This is the licensed day care aspect of Katutura Youth Centre. Every Day the youth receive snacks and meals. During the program, we also offer enrichment activities Programs we offer are offering Karate, Flag Football, Cycling, Mindfulness, Yoga, Aqua Fitness, Healthy Living, Life skills, Cooking, Baking, Dance, Photography, Art, STEM, Overcoming Obstacles as well as Literacy (iready) and homework help.

Train Lead Succeed

Our TLS (Train Lead Succeed) Program is for youth middle school on up! The youth work on career exploration, life skills, mentoring, dance, healthy living, cycling, recreation, art, cooking, yoga, homework help, literacy work and all around support and resources for the young people in the program.

Community and Enrichment Activities

Weekly on Wednesdays, the youth center offers meals for families to come and dine with us and the kids. During the summer we offer weekly community bbq and bike rides. Thursday community Girls Rides with the Katutura Front Ranger Cycling Club and the Katutura Police Female officers. We also offer community support for space usage, community meetings, holiday support, back to school supplies, and other items as donations and funding allow.
