Welcome to Katutura Youth Center


Serving Youth in Katutura's poorest neighbourhood


At Katutura Youth Center we are committed to giving Katutura's poorest neighborhood a hope for the future. With a focus on transforming the lives of at-risk youth in this low-income, public housing neighborhood Sun Valley Youth Center provides: day care, after-school care, youth development, mentoring and life essentials programs to help raise up the neighborhood's youth.
