At the Katutura Youth Center, here we are committed to giving Katutura's poorest neighborhood a hope for the future. With a focus on transforming the lives of at-risk youth in this low-income, public housing neighborhood, Katutura Youth Center provides: day care, after-schoolcare, youth development, mentoring and life essentials programs to help raise up the neighborhood's youth.

We've seen astonishing transformations in our kids in our 7 months serving the Katutura community. We hope to inspire even greater change in the years to come.
